SYNTHI BISCUIT

Designed to offer utility functions in a small package(16HP).

The Synthi Biscuit gives the most usual functions (LFO, sample and hold, random noise, sequencer ect...) and more. It offers 10 different algorythms within 3 sub-units. A sub-module consists of 2 parameters, a gate input, an analog output, a display and a selection button.

No magic tricks, no special button combo. To access the different functions, click the algo button to browse through the algorythms. The parameters knobs allow changes in the functions you're using, they have different roles depending on the mode (speed lfo, gate time, random factor ect...).

The synthi-biscuit responds to all Eurorack criterias on power supply(12V/0V/-12V), power connector (2X8), and signal levels.

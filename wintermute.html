WINTERMUTE



Inspired by several breed of noise machine exposed in the matrix and designed at first for pure silliness, the noise machine "wintermute" proved to be an interesting and enjoyable tool for sonic mayhem.

It consists of three free-running or synced oscillators switchable to audio or LFO range, a ring modulator, a color control, and a filter

Wintermute is available bare naked, only its pcb and it's plastic body is available.
